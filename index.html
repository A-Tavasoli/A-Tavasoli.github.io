Ali Tavasoli

Assistant Professor, Department of Mathematics & Statistics, James Madison University

My research centers on adaptive and nonlinear control, nonlinear dynamics and chaos, and online optimization and learning in feedback systems. I build on foundational theories in nonlinear control, evolution equations, and dynamical systems, and extend them to address emerging challenges in modern mechanical engineering, particularly in complex vehicle subsystems.
