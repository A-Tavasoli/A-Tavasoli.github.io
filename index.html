About

I am an Assistant Professor at James Madison University. My research focuses on dynamical systems, control, optimization, and learning-based methods, with applications including automated insulin delivery and complex networked systems.

Research

Adaptive and Nonlinear Control

Control design, stability analysis, and online adaptation for nonlinear systems.

Dynamical Systems and Operator-Theoretic Methods

Koopman-based methods, structure-aware modeling, and data-driven dynamics.

Diabetes Technology

Algorithms for personalized and automated insulin delivery systems.
